How RISET DAO Works

Every RISET transaction attracts a fee of 1% into the DAO Treasury. If the DAO treasury accumulates a RISET value of 200 ETHW or more, 170 ETHW of RISET is sent to the next approved proposal and 30 ETHW of RISET is sent to the burn wallet (burnt). A proposal is submitted by a Patriot on behalf of other crusaders since Patriots are the only ones allowed to create proposals. A Team/Company needing funding from RISET DAO will have to agree to liquidate a percentage of thier token to RISET. 50% of this token will be distributed to RISET Stakers while the remaining 50% is sent to burn address.
